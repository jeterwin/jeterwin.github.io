_Casual Survival (3D)

Forest's Lullaby

1 / 3
2 / 3
3 / 3
❮ ❯

Development Period: January 2024 - February 2024


Short Description: Forest Lullaby is a survival game that aims to be both realistic and casual. The gameplay is set in a forest and it requires the player to be wary of multiple realistic mechanics: a food, hydration and warmness system, which all affect the gameplay of the player by inducing screen effects and/or debuffs, for example a freezing screen and less movement when freezing. On top of these mechanics, the players can hunt down animals with a variety of weapons, craft buildings and craft armor in order to protect themselves.


The atmosphere and graphics of the game are made in an aesthethically pleasing way, in order to calm and relax the players. It is a first person casual survival game
